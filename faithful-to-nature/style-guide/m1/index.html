01 — Colours

02 — Typography

03 — Buttons

Buttons

FTN M1 uses flat, minimal buttons. All radii are 4px — no pills, no heavy rounding. The primary CTA is "Add to basket" (not "Add to cart"). Button text uses Open Sans at 18px weight 400 — notably not bold.
