Key differences vs M1
The M2 site is a major visual departure from M1. Page background is #F4F2ED (warm parchment — not white). Heading colour shifts from olive #353222 to dark teal #204B4A. Price colour changes from black/grey to #D46327 rust. Body font switches from Open Sans to proxima-nova at 16px (not 13px). The overall feel is more editorial and premium.
01 — Colours

Colour System

M2 retains the core FTN green but introduces a warmer, more editorial palette. The parchment page background and rust price colour give a premium, nature-editorial feel distinct from M1's flat green aesthetic.

02 — Typography

Typography

M2 uses proxima-nova (a clean humanist sans) for all body and UI text, and the fortescue serif family for editorial headings. Body text is 16px (vs 13px on M1) — a significantly more spacious reading experience.

03 — Buttons

Buttons

M2 buttons are slightly larger than M1 (16px text, 44px height) with the same 4px radius. Font weight is 600 (vs 400 on M1). The primary CTA remains "Add to basket".
