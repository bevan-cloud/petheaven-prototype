Product Prioritization

Two complementary frameworks for evaluating Faithful to Nature's product backlog. Use the RICE table to score initiatives quantitatively, or switch to the Impact vs Effort matrix for a visual portfolio view. All scores are editable.

R
Reach
How many users are affected? Scale 1–10
I
Impact
How much does it move the metric? Scale 1–5
C
Confidence
How certain are we? Scale 1–5
E
Effort
Build cost — higher = more work. Scale 1–5
RICE = (R × I × C) ÷ E · higher score = higher priority · all values editable
Click any R / I / C / E value in the table to edit it. Press Enter or Tab to save, Esc to cancel.
