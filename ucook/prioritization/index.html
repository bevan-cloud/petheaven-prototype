All UCook product initiatives scored using the RICE framework. Click any R / I / C / E score to edit it — the RICE score recalculates instantly.

R
Reach
How many users are affected? Scale 1–10
I
Impact
How much does it move the metric? Scale 1–5
C
Confidence
How certain are we? Scale 1–5
E
Effort
Build cost (higher = more work). Scale 1–5
RICE = (R × I × C) ÷ E · higher is better · all scores editable
