04 — Logo
Logo & Usage

The UCook wordmark is a clean, bold SVG — always used at full colour or white. Never stretch, rotate, recolour, or place it on backgrounds without sufficient contrast.

Primary — on dark
White version on #1E1E1E · footer / hero overlays
On white
Full-colour on #FFFFFF · nav bar / cards
On brand orange
White version on #FF6803 · promotional banners
Asset
Format: SVG (vector — scales to any size)
Reference size: 68 × 29 px
https://www.ucook.co.za/logos/large.svg
Minimum clear space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "U" letterform on all sides. Never crowd the logo with other elements.
✓ Do
  • Use white logo on dark or orange backgrounds
  • Use full-colour logo on white or very light surfaces
  • Maintain the SVG aspect ratio at all sizes
  • Use on-brand backgrounds only (#1E1E1E, #FFFFFF, #FF6803)
✗ Don't
  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Recolour the logo in non-brand colours
  • Place on low-contrast or patterned backgrounds
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Use as a watermark at reduced opacity
