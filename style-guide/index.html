04 — Logo
Logo & Usage

The Pet Heaven logo is a horizontal lockup — white paw print icon + "PET HEAVEN" wordmark. It is exclusively used on the brand green header background. A dark version is used for print and email contexts.

Primary — on PH Green
Site header · #50B848 background · always white version
On dark footer
Footer & dark overlays · #3B3B3B background
Dark version on light
Email / print · #FFFFFF or #F9F9F9 surfaces only
Asset
Format: PNG · 300 × 73 px
petheaven.co.za/skin/frontend/petheaven/default/images/logo-small.png
Composition
White paw print icon + "PET HEAVEN" wordmark in Poppins. Maintain the icon-to-text proportions — never use the icon or wordmark in isolation.
Minimum clear space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the paw print icon on all sides. The logo must never be crowded by navigation items, imagery, or other UI elements.
✓ Do
  • Always use the white logo on green (#50B848) and dark (#3B3B3B) backgrounds
  • Use the dark/inverted version for print and email
  • Keep the icon and wordmark together as a lockup
  • Display at the reference size of 300 × 73 px or proportionally scaled
✗ Don't
  • Place the white logo on a white or light background
  • Recolour the logo in purple (#BD08C3), lime, or any non-brand colour
  • Stretch, crop, or rotate the lockup
  • Use the paw print icon without the wordmark
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or opacity effects
